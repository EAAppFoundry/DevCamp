So What is DevCamp?

A month-long event focused on innovation and development

A dozen or so developers break up into teams...

...come up with product ideas...

...and pitch those ideas to their fellow attendees...

The best ideas move forward and are implemented

How it Works

Each session consists of 10-20 members

The number of teams is dynamic; between 2-4

Attendees are encouraged to take part in TBD Labs for the duration of DevCamp

They can expect to spend additional hours working on their projects. To help, Turner will donate 3 hours/week.

Plus attendees will participate in a one-week hackathon

At the end of the "semester", each team delivers a final presentation and demo's their app

The Hackathon

  • We go off-site to get away from work and distractions
  • 5 Days
  • The goal is to get as much of the project completed as possible

The Final Presentation

At the final presentation, attendees pitch their ideas and demo the resulting product. This allows developers to gain experience explaining technical topics to a non-technical audience.
